About me

Embarking on my professional journey during my pursuit of a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, I discovered a deep-seated passion for design. Despite completing my degree, I found myself unsatisfied, driven instead by a desire to delve into the realms of web design and development. Taking the initiative, I began immersing myself in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Through self-directed learning and project implementation, I honed my skills and cultivated a profound interest in web development. Each project presented its own set of challenges, encouraging me to push boundaries and deepen my understanding of these technologies.

Seeking to formalize my expertise, I enrolled in Google's UI/UX professional certification course, marking the genesis of my journey into UX design. This comprehensive program provided invaluable insights into the nuances of user experience research and prototyping, further refining my skills.

Now, I am driven to establish myself as a highly skilled and reliable team member in the dynamic realm of web development.
